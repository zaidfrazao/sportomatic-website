Get to know Sportomatic

We're a young, proudly South African tech startup with big dreams and the drive to make those dreams a reality.

Vision Statement

Sportomatic will transform the way sports teams gather, manage, distribute and communicate information. This will be done by providing innovative, interactive, and consumer-driven sports management software which streamlines those tedious admin tasks associated with managing a team.

Mission
