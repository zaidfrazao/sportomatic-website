Features that streamline our sports management.

Sportomatic has a variety features that allow your sports coordinators to spend less time on the admin and more time of their teams.

  • Notifications

Sign Up Free

Your entire sports calendar in your pocket.

At the start of your season, you quickly set up all fixture and practice times. We'll remind you weekly to fill in details for the upcoming week's schedule.

Your managers and coaches will receive that week's schedule, knowing exactly where they need to be, when they need to be there.

Should there be any changes, Sportomatic will make sure that relevant people are notified.

Track exactly how much you owe your coaches.

Sportomatic has a unique hour logging system that logs coaches in when they show up for practice and logs them out when they leave. This means that you will always know exactly how much to pay each of your coaches, with other features that stretch this even further.

Everyone gets to use Sportomatic for free.

You pay the fee and your staff get the software completely free. This includes the iOS, Android and web apps. And you can have as many staff accounts as you need!

We'll make sure you don't forget the important stuff.

Sportomatic has a notification system that let's managers and coaches know when they have to be at fixtures, alerts you of important reports that require your attention and reminds you to pay your coaches.

Regularly receive summaries of how things are going.

Sportomatic generates weekly reports summarising hours logged, wages incurred as a results, and any unusual activity, like a coach completely missing a practice or a manager giving 4 hours of overtime.

These reports get stored securely on the Cloud and are accessible at any time. They can also be printed and emailed for your convenience.

Track trends to optimise results.

It's often difficult to get a feel for the bigger picture. Do you need more coaches because you're spending too much on paying overtime? During which season do you spend the most money and why? How often are coaches missing practices?

Sportomatic will generate graphical representations of the data it track so that it's easier for you to answer these kinds of questions.

Automated billing organised and stored safely on the Cloud.

We want to make paying your coaches as hastle-free as possible, so we made Sportomatic capable of taking all the of the hours logging info and automatically creating bills that you can send to your coaches with the click of a button.

Sportomatic also exports the hour logging data into an Excel-friendly format so that your accounting department can easily integrate it into the books.

Sportomatic is an evolving product.

Right now, Sportomatic is the best product on the market when it comes to streamlining the way you pay your coaching staff, but we want it to be so much more!

Without giving too much away, we will be expanding the suite to turn it into the complete package when it comes to managing all aspects of sports management, especially communication and performance tracking

We also want to allow your athletes to get in on the fun with their own versions of the app. All for free!

Whatever the future holds, you can be sure that as Sportomatic grows as a company, so too will Sportomatic grow as a product!
